Background
Ran

I started this podcast because I was tired of design content that stopped at the craft layer. The real conversations — about strategy, about business, about getting your work to matter — were happening in DMs and dinner tables, not in public. So I decided to make them public.

The Journey

12 Years
in design.

2011
The Beginning

Fell into
design.

Started as a visual designer with zero formal training. Figured it out by shipping things and breaking things — and realised design was the closest thing to magic I'd ever found.

2014
Chartboost

Designed for
scale.

Joined the mobile gaming ad tech world. Learned that great design isn't just beautiful — it moves numbers. First taste of design that's accountable to the business.

2017
Adobe

Designed at
the source.

Worked on tools that designers themselves used every day. The pressure of that feedback loop — designing for the world's most critical design audience — sharpened everything.

2019
Amplitude

Learned to
speak data.

Embedded with product and growth teams at a fast-moving analytics company. Design had to earn its seat at the table — so I learned to argue in metrics, not just pixels.

2021
Shopify

Reached
staff.

Made Staff Designer at one of the world's most influential commerce platforms. Stopped being just an individual contributor and started shaping how entire product areas thought about design.

2023
The Wall

Hit burnout.
Hard.

Twelve-hour days caught up. Took a step back, questioned everything, and rebuilt my relationship with work from scratch. Best thing that ever happened to my career — and my life.

2024
AirOps · Ran Talks Design

Started the
conversation.

Joined AirOps as Staff Designer to build the future of AI-powered work. And launched this podcast — because the real conversations about design, strategy, and growth needed to happen in public.
